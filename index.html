IS 201: Introduction to Information Systems | Demonstrations

IS 201 is the Introduction to Information Systems class at Brigham Young University. It is taught during the Fall, Winter, Spring, and Summer semesters. The course teaches how to create technology solutions to solve business problems. Hands-on coursework includes databases, analytics, automation, and programming skills that are useful to all business majors.
